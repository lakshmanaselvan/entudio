STOCK MANAGEMENT

Stock management, also known as inventory management, refers to the process of overseeing and controlling the flow of goods or products within a business. It involves the management of stock levels, ordering and replenishment, storage, and tracking of inventory to ensure optimal availability and minimize costs.
